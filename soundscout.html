SoundScout – AI Powered Smart Glasses

IEEE EPICS Project

SoundScout is an AI-powered assistive wearable designed for visually and hearing-impaired individuals. It detects environmental sounds in real-time and provides haptic feedback to improve safety and situational awareness.

  • Real-time sound detection using AI
  • Haptic feedback for alerts
  • Assistive technology for social impact

Problem Statement

Individuals with sensory impairments often struggle to detect important environmental sounds such as alarms, vehicle horns, and approaching people, which can pose safety risks.

Proposed Solution

SoundScout uses AI-based sound classification to identify critical sounds and convert them into vibration patterns, enabling users to respond without relying on visual or auditory cues.

Technologies Used

  • Machine Learning for sound classification
  • Microphone sensors
  • Haptic motors
  • Embedded processing unit

Impact

The project enhances independence, mobility, and safety for individuals with disabilities and demonstrates the use of AI for social good.
